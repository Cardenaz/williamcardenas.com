The Genus of Angular Momentum

Dec 12, 2025

What is the genus of angular momentum?

We want a definition on the form of:

Angular momentum is a X with the differentia Y.

But it is not self-evident to me what exactly it should be.

We know that it is a physical quantity, but this might be too wide of a category.

Suggestion 1: Angular momentum is a physical quantity that describes rotational motion.

This can’t be proper since, e.g., define $L = r \times v$, and that would also describe rotational motion but not be angular momentum. So the definition is missing an inertial quantity.
